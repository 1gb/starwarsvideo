Star wars dot video is a curated collection of short Star Wars related videos found on YouTube. Many people all over the world have been moved by this franchise in a way that has inspired them to create their own works of art. This website is meant to be an exploration and celebration of this tiny aspect of the Star Wars fandom.

One day the universe may know peace between Jedi and Sith, but until then please enjoy this dumb website.
